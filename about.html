Our Skill

At QAIT DevLabs we create innovative products and solutions for our customers. We look at ourselves not only as technology partners of our customers but as their innovation partners.

We specialize in ideating with business users, customers and suggest elegant technical solutions to their most complex problems.


As much as we like to ideate and develop concrete requirements, we thrive on rigorous engineering to produce software that generates a “wow” experience for the end users, which translates to having all of the given in any product – rich features that always work correctly, intuitiveness and high degree of usability, faster performance and robust security.


Our Philosophy

Why is it that certain products are hugely successful while others even though engineered well, fail to lure users?


Yes, it is about building the right features, timing, pricing/business model and several other factors.

However, a differentiating factor in today’s world is intelligence and simplicity. That is software that is smart yet simple and intuitive to use.
We preach and practice this throughout our software development efforts. We believe that great software products get built not just based on the idea behind them, but also thoughtful and meticulous execution of the idea. And at QAIT DevLabs we stand true to this philosophy.


Our infrastructure

At QA InfoTech, and QAIT DevLabs in particular, we have taken special care to provide our team and clients with best of breed hardware and software development tools. Our labs are geared with range of Wintel, MACs and Linux boxes and various server class machines. Our mobile development team is armed with iOS and Android based tablets along with selected phones of these as well as other platforms (Nokia, Blackberry).


Our network architecture is robust and complimented with high availability servers, switches, firewalls, VPN tunnels for remote client access and RAID based terabyte storage.


Our product engineering backbone includes – technology/language specific IDEs, SVN and GIT code versioning systems, various CI systems such as Hudson, web and application servers such as Apache, Tomcat, JBoss etc, database servers – Oracle, MS SQL Server, MySQL and very many other software packages and tools.
